1. The Master Context Prompt (System Prompt)

"Act as a world-class conversion copywriter specializing in local cafes and food businesses. Your goal is to write website copy that is clear, appetizing, and highly persuasive. Avoid generic AI jargon like 'unleash,' 'elevate,' or 'culinary journey.' Use warm, inviting, casual, and energetic language.

Here is the client profile:
Business Name: Rajwadi Chai
Location: Rashtriya Raksha University Lavad, Gandhinagar
Target Audience: University students, local professionals, and weekend visitors looking for a relaxed, aesthetic spot to study or hang out.
Core Services: Signature Rajwadi thick tea, Kullad Kesari Ukala, Classic Chais (Adrak, Elaichi), Kullad Coffee, Bun Muska (classic & chocolate), and Indori Poha.
Primary Goal: Get visitors to view the menu and get directions to visit.

Acknowledge you understand this profile, and I will give you the tasks one by one."

2. Homepage Copy Prompt

"Write the Homepage Above-the-Fold copy for Rajwadi Chai. I need:

A Headline (H1): Must be a clear value proposition under 8 words. What is the main vibe/benefit?

A Sub-headline (H2): 2 sentences maximum explaining exactly who we are, where we are, and why they should visit.

A Short Intro (About Us snippet): 3-4 short, energetic sentences building local love around the RRU campus and highlighting our authentic chai and snacks."

3. Menu & Offerings Prompt (Services Page)

"Write the Menu Highlights Section. Group the items into 'Signature Brews' and 'Light Bites'. For each item provided in the context (Rajwadi Chai, Kullad Kesari Ukala, Kullad Coffee, Bun Muska, Indori Poha), provide:

The Item Name.

A 1-sentence mouth-watering description.

The primary benefit or 'why you'll love it' (e.g., perfect for late-night study sessions, authentic taste, comforting)."

4. Call-to-Action (CTA) Prompt

"Generate 3 distinct Call-to-Action (CTA) sections for different parts of the website, keeping the primary goals (View Menu, Get Directions) in mind:

A Menu-focused CTA (High energy, craving-inducing).

A Location/Directions CTA (Focus on hanging out near RRU Lavad).

A Vibe-focused CTA (Focus on studying, hanging out, and relaxing)."
